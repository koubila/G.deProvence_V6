About

I am Graziella Perron, Founder of G. de Provence, a premium House of Provençal cosmetics and interior fragrances, created for Palaces, Spas, and Fine Dining establishments worldwide.

After leading for many years the structure and expansion of a European financial empire, I am now building G. de Provence — an architectural vision of premium cosmetics and fragrances, where the discipline of major institutions meets the raw poetry of my adopted land.

For me, Provence is not merely a destination; it is an anchor from which I draw my inspiration. This territory, admired across the world, deserves an ambassador who understands its secrets and honors its craftsmanship.

G. de Provence is a House conceived for longevity, structured for excellence, and led with the boldness of those who know that the impossible does not exist. I have built this House so that every detail is a note of poetry and every decision a solid stone laid for decades to come.

I invite you to step into this universe of light, Provençal fragrances, and refined well-being.
